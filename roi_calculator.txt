Accidents per year: 5
Average repair cost per accident: $1,500
Current simulator cost: $10,000 per unit

ANNUAL COSTS BEFORE SIMULATOR:
-------------------------------
Fuel costs:
  10 vehicles × $20/day × 250 days = $50,000

Maintenance costs:
  10 vehicles × $3,000/year = $30,000

Insurance costs:
  10 vehicles × $2,000/year = $20,000

Repair costs:
  5 accidents × $1,500 = $7,500

TOTAL ANNUAL COSTS: $107,500

ANNUAL COSTS WITH SIMULATOR:
-----------------------------
Assume 50% of training hours shift to simulator:

Fuel costs (50% reduction):
  $50,000 × 50% = $25,000

Maintenance costs (50% reduction):
  $30,000 × 50% = $15,000

Insurance costs (50% reduction):
  $20,000 × 50% = $10,000

Repair costs (90% reduction - simulator is zero risk):
  $7,500 × 10% = $750

Simulator costs (1 unit + maintenance):
  Purchase: $10,000 (one-time)
  Annual maintenance: $500

TOTAL ANNUAL COSTS: $51,250

SAVINGS:
---------
Annual savings: $107,500 - $51,250 = $56,250
ROI period: $10,000 ÷ $56,250 = 0.2 years (approximately 2.5 months)

5-year total savings: $56,250 × 5 - $10,000 = $271,250
